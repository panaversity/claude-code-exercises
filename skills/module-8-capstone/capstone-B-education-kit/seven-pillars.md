# The Seven Pillars of AI-Driven Development
(Abbreviated — see Module 4 Exercise 4.3 for full version)

1. **Problem Decomposition** — Breaking vague goals into clear, specific tasks
2. **Specification Writing** — Describing desired outcomes precisely
3. **Context Engineering** — Providing the right background for AI agents
4. **Quality Verification** — Evaluating AI output critically
5. **Iterative Refinement** — Improving through specific feedback cycles
6. **Tool Orchestration** — Selecting, configuring, and combining AI tools
7. **Responsible AI Practice** — Using AI ethically and safely
