## 1. Disclosure
All content substantially generated by AI must include a disclosure statement. Acceptable forms:
- "This content was created with AI assistance."
- A footnote indicating AI tools were used in drafting
- The content does NOT need to disclose if AI was used only for proofreading/grammar

## 2. Accuracy
All factual claims in AI-generated content must be verified by a human before publication.
- Statistics must include their source
- Quotes must be verified as real (AI can fabricate quotes)
- Technical claims must be reviewed by a subject matter expert

## 3. No Fabricated Attribution
AI-generated content must NOT:
- Attribute quotes to real people unless verified
- Cite sources that don't exist
- Create fake testimonials or endorsements

## 4. Data Privacy
AI-generated content must NOT:
- Include personal information of employees or customers
- Reference specific customer data, even anonymized if identifiable
- Include internal company metrics unless approved for public release

## 5. Bias and Fairness
AI-generated content must:
- Be reviewed for stereotypes or biased language
- Avoid gendered language when referring to roles (use "they" not "he/she")
- Not make assumptions about demographics

## 6. Brand Consistency
AI-generated content must:
- Follow the company style guide
- Use approved terminology
- Match the tone appropriate for the channel (formal for external, casual for internal)

## 7. Human Review
All AI-generated content intended for external publication must be reviewed and approved by at least one human editor before release.
