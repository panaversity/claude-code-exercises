# Channel Formatting Guidelines

## Blog Post
- 600-800 words
- H1 title, H2 subheadings every 150-200 words
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Include at least one concrete example or data point
- End with a clear call to action
- No emojis

## LinkedIn Post
- 150-250 words per post
- Hook in the first line (this is what shows before "see more")
- Use line breaks for readability
- Can include 2-3 relevant hashtags
- Can include 1-2 emojis sparingly
- End with a question or CTA

## Twitter/X Thread
- 5-7 tweets
- First tweet must stand alone and hook readers
- Each tweet under 280 characters
- Thread should tell a complete story

## Email Newsletter
- 100 words maximum
- Subject line + preview text
- One key takeaway
- One link/CTA
- Casual-professional tone
