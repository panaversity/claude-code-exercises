# Content Brief: The Future of AI Education

**Topic:** How AI-native education is different from traditional tech education — and why it matters

**Key Points:**
- Traditional: Learn syntax → build projects → get job
- AI-native: Learn to specify → orchestrate AI → verify output → ship products
- The role of the student shifts from "coder" to "director"
- Success metric changes: not "can you write the code?" but "can you get the right outcome?"
- Panaversity's approach: 16,000+ learners, hands-on exercises, real-world projects

**Target Audience:** Students considering tech careers, educators, and training managers

**Tone:** Visionary but grounded in evidence, avoid being preachy
