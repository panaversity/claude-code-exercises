# Small Business AI Case Studies

## Case Study 1: Local Bakery (8 employees)
**Use case:** AI-generated social media posts + automated email marketing
**Investment:** $100/month (Canva AI + Mailchimp)
**Results:** Social media engagement up 200%, email list grew 45% in 3 months
**Challenge:** Getting the tone right took 2 weeks of iteration
**Quote:** "It's like having a marketing person but at $100/month instead of $4,000/month"

## Case Study 2: Accounting Firm (15 employees)
**Use case:** AI for first-pass document review and data extraction from receipts
**Investment:** $500/month (Claude Pro + custom integration)
**Results:** Processing time cut by 60%, fewer manual errors
**Challenge:** Required 1 month of setup and training. Senior partners were skeptical at first.
**Quote:** "The junior accountants embraced it immediately. The seniors took longer but now they love it."
