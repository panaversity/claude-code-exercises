# Small Business AI Adoption Survey — January 2025
Based on interviews with 50 small business owners.

**Adoption Rate:** 42% use at least one AI tool (up from 18% in 2023)

**Most Common Use Cases:**
1. Customer service chatbots — 35% of AI-adopting businesses
2. Content creation — 28%
3. Bookkeeping/invoicing — 24%
4. Marketing optimization — 22%
5. Data analysis — 18%

**Average Time to ROI:** 2-3 months for most use cases

**Top Barriers:**
1. Don't know which tools to adopt — 62%
2. Privacy/security concerns — 54%
3. Lack of staff expertise — 48%
4. Cost concerns — 31%
5. "We tried it, didn't work" — 22%

**Budget Allocation:**
- Average monthly AI tool spend: $150-500
- 65% using only free tiers or trials
- 20% paying for premium subscriptions
- 15% investing in custom solutions
