# Future of Work: Key Predictions

Saved from McKinsey Report, January 2025

- By 2030, 30% of work hours could be automated with current AI technology
- Knowledge workers will be most affected in the short term
- New job categories: AI trainers, prompt engineers, AI ethicists
- Hybrid human-AI teams outperform either alone by 40%
- The biggest bottleneck is organizational change, not technology
