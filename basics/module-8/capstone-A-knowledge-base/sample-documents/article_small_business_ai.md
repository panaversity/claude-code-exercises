# Small Business AI Adoption: What Actually Works

Based on interviews with 50 small business owners who adopted AI tools in 2024.

What worked:
- Starting with ONE specific, repetitive task (not "transform everything")
- Customer service chatbots (simple FAQ handling)
- Content creation for social media
- Bookkeeping and invoice processing
- Email drafting and follow-ups

What didn't work:
- Trying to automate sales conversations
- Using AI for strategic decisions without human review
- Implementing without any training
- Expecting perfection from day one

ROI timeline: Most saw meaningful time savings within 2-3 months.
Average time saved: 8-12 hours per week for the business owner.
