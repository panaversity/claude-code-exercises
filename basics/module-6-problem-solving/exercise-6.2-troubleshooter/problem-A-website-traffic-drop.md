# Problem A: Website Traffic Dropped 40%

## The Situation
You run an e-commerce website selling handmade crafts. Last month, your website traffic dropped by 40% compared to the previous month. Revenue is down 35%.

## What You Know
- The drop happened gradually over 3 weeks (not sudden)
- Your main traffic sources were Google organic search (60%) and Instagram (25%)
- You redesigned the website 6 weeks ago (new look, faster loading)
- Google Search Console shows impressions are down
- You didn't change your Instagram posting schedule
- A competitor launched a major Black Friday campaign last month
- Your hosting provider had two brief outages (30 min each)

## What You DON'T Know
- Whether the site redesign affected SEO
- If Google changed its algorithm recently
- Whether Instagram changed their algorithm
- If your email list engagement has changed
- Whether the competitor is specifically targeting your keywords
