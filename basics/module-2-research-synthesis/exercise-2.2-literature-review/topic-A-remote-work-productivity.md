# Topic A: Remote Work Productivity

## Background
Since 2020, remote work has become mainstream. But the research on whether it helps or hurts productivity is surprisingly mixed. Some studies show productivity gains; others show decline. The answer seems to depend heavily on context.

## Angles to Explore
- How is "productivity" defined differently across studies?
- What types of work benefit most from remote settings?
- What role does management style play?
- How do results differ for new employees vs. experienced ones?
- What does the latest data (2024-2025) show vs. early pandemic data?

## Interesting Tensions
- Individual productivity vs. team collaboration
- Short-term output vs. long-term innovation
- Employee satisfaction vs. company performance metrics
