# Course Brief: AI Tools for Everyday Productivity

## Course Philosophy
This is NOT a technical course. Students won't learn how AI works internally. Instead, they'll learn how to USE AI tools to be more productive in their existing jobs.

## The 5 Lessons (Suggested, feel free to adjust)

### Lesson 1: "Your AI Assistant" (Foundation)
- What AI tools are and aren't
- Setting up Claude and/or ChatGPT
- Your first conversation: asking good questions
- The difference between vague and specific prompts

### Lesson 2: "Writing & Communication" (Applied)
- Using AI for email drafting
- Meeting notes and summaries
- Report writing assistance
- When AI writing is appropriate vs. inappropriate

### Lesson 3: "Data & Analysis" (Applied)
- Analyzing spreadsheets with AI
- Creating charts and visualizations
- Summarizing long documents
- Basic data cleaning and organization

### Lesson 4: "Workflow Automation" (Advanced)
- Identifying tasks that can be automated
- Using AI for batch operations (renaming, organizing, templating)
- Creating reusable prompts
- Introduction to Cowork for file-based tasks

### Lesson 5: "Putting It All Together" (Capstone)
- Building a personal AI workflow
- Quality control and fact-checking
- AI ethics and responsible use
- Creating your own "AI playbook"

## Assessment
Each lesson should have a practical exercise, and the final quiz should test both knowledge and application.
