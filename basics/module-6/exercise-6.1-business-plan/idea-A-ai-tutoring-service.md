# Business Idea A: AI-Powered Tutoring Service

## The Concept
An online tutoring service that combines AI tools with human tutors. Students get 24/7 AI help for homework and practice, plus weekly 1-on-1 sessions with a human tutor for deeper learning.

## Target Market
High school and college students preparing for exams (SAT, GRE, professional certifications).

## Initial Thoughts
- Price point: $99/month for AI + 2 human sessions, $49/month for AI-only
- First subjects: Math, Science, English
- Start with 5 part-time tutors
- Build a simple web platform

## What You Haven't Figured Out Yet
- How to find and vet tutors
- What AI tools to use (build custom or use existing)
- How to measure student progress
- Marketing strategy on a small budget
- Legal requirements for working with minors
